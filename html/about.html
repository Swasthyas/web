Workplace Enhanced Wellness And Mental Health Services

WE-WHAMS is designed to bring together enhanced, inclusive, and innovative solutions for mental well-being at work. It focuses on supporting employees through tailored services, group activities, and wellness programs that promote a healthier, happier, and more productive workplace.

Workplace Mental Health Services


At Swasthyas - The Self-Care Adda, we believe that mental well-being is the foundation of a thriving workplace. Our Workplace Mental Health Services are thoughtfully designed to foster a positive and supportive environment where employees can grow, collaborate, and achieve their best.

Why Workplace Mental Health Matters

In today’s high-pressure work culture, stress, burnout, and mental health challenges are increasingly common. By prioritizing mental well-being, organizations can unlock the full potential of their teams, improving productivity, morale, and overall workplace harmony.

Our Services:


1. Workshop : will be conducted both offline and online, covering 20 key topics relevant to corporate lifestyles. These workshops are designed to address essential areas such as stress management, anxiety, resilience, work-life balance, conflict resolution, and effectiveness. Each session is interactive and tailored to empower employees with practical tools to navigate challenges, improve well-being, and thrive in professional environments.

2. Psychometric Evaluation: assesses employees' stress levels, resilience, and work-life balance using validated tools, providing actionable insights to enhance well-being and productivity within the organization.

3. One on One sessions : provide employees with a safe and confidential space to address personal or professional challenges. These personalized counseling sessions focus on improving mental well-being, building resilience, and equipping individuals with tailored strategies to manage stress, anxiety, work-life balance, and other concerns effectively.

4. Mental Health Kit : is a thoughtfully curated set of resources and tools designed to promote well-being and help employees manage stress and emotional challenges.

5. 24/7 TrainingAssistance: This service ensures round-the-clock support for employees and team leads, providing immediate guidance and resources on workplace mental health.

6. Training: Equips leaders with skills to recognize and address mental health concerns, foster effective communication, and create a supportive work environment that enhances team well-being and productivity.

7. Generalized Monthly Reports: Generalized Monthly Reports are concise summaries of the Workshop, Psychometric Evaluation, 1 on 1 session, Recharge session and training.

8. Recharge session :A 15-minute mindfulness or activity-based session designed to refresh and energize employees. Conducted mid-workday or just before a meeting, these sessions aim to reduce stress, improve focus, and boost team morale.

9. Crisis Intervention & Support: This service provides immediate assistance during workplace mental health emergencies, ensuring timely and compassionate care for employees in distress

Our Approach:
Our team of experienced psychologists and mental health professionals combines an eclectic, trauma-informed approach with practical tools for stress relief. We tailor our services to your organization’s unique culture and challenges, ensuring that mental health support seamlessly integrates into the workplace.
