WE-WHAMS

Workplace Enhanced Wellness And Mental Health Services

WE-WHAMS is designed to bring together enhanced, inclusive, and innovative solutions for mental well-being at work. It focuses on supporting employees through tailored services, group activities, and wellness programs that promote a healthier, happier, and more productive workplace.

Workplace Mental Health Services


At Swasthyas - The Self-Care Adda, we believe that mental well-being is the foundation of a thriving workplace. Our Workplace Mental Health Services are thoughtfully designed to foster a positive and supportive environment where employees can grow, collaborate, and achieve their best.

Why Workplace Mental Health Matters

In today’s high-pressure work culture, stress, burnout, and mental health challenges are increasingly common. By prioritizing mental well-being, organizations can unlock the full potential of their teams, improving productivity, morale, and overall workplace harmony.

Our Services:


1. Workshops & Training Programs:
- Stress Management Techniques
- Mindfulness & Emotional Regulation
- Building Resilience at Work
- Conflict Resolution and Communication Skills

2. Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs)
- Private one-on-one counseling sessions
- 24/7 access to online mental health support
- Confidential assistance with both personal and workplace concerns

3. Leadership & Management Training
- Developing empathetic leadership
- Enhancing team-building and communication skills
- Recognizing and addressing mental health concerns in teams

4. Group Sessions and Relaxation Activities
Themed Group Therapy: Address common concerns such as stress, burnout, or work-life balance in guided group sessions.
Creative Expression Workshops: Engage in art, music, or expressive writing to unwind and discover new ways of coping.
Guided Meditation & Yoga Classes: Regular sessions to promote mindfulness, relaxation, and physical well-being.
Recreational Team Activities: Activities like laughter therapy, team games, or even storytelling circles to foster bonding and reduce stress.

5. Mental Health Assessments & Check-Ins
- Scheduled well-being evaluations
- Customized surveys to identify specific needs

6. Crisis Intervention & Support
- On-site and remote crisis support - Post-crisis recovery sessions for teams


Our Approach:
Our team of experienced psychologists and mental health professionals combines an eclectic, trauma-informed approach with practical tools for stress relief. We tailor our services to your organization’s unique culture and challenges, ensuring that mental health support seamlessly integrates into the workplace.

The Benefits of Partnering with Swasthyas:

Boost Employee Engagement: A healthier team is a more productive and creative team.
Reduce Burnout: Equip employees with tools to manage stress and prevent exhaustion.
Foster a Culture of Care: Build trust and a sense of belonging within your organization.
