Therapist

Harshitha Prabhu,

Therapist

Harshitha Prabhu, with over 3,000 hours of counseling, specializes in supporting clients across all age groups with concerns like anxiety, depression, stress, gender identity, academics, career counseling, and couple therapy. Using approaches like CBT, Freudian techniques, and Person-Centered Therapy, she creates a safe, compassionate space for healing and growth. Harshitha believes in the power of self-healing and reminds clients, "It's okay to not be okay; it's okay to seek help."

Sonam R Wadkar,

Therapist

Sonam R Wadkar, a psychologist who specializes in psychotherapy, educational and personal growth, relationship issues, and managing anxiety, depression, and stress. With a psychodynamic approach, she fosters a safe and supportive space for healing. Sonam’s empathetic and open-minded nature, combined with her authentic and strategic methods, helps clients build resilience and clarity. She believes, “I am thinking. Therefore, I exist.”

Yeshyeshwini Gopinathan,

Therapist

Yeshyeshwini Gopinathan, a psychologist and mental health advocate who specializes in trauma, relationships, anxiety, addiction, and productivity. With dual Master’s degrees in Clinical and Developmental Psychology and certifications in Expressive Arts Therapy and NLP, she creates a safe, judgment-free space to support clients in becoming their best selves. Using an eclectic and trauma-focused approach, Yeshyeshwini empowers clients to navigate challenges, achieve emotional well-being, and lead fulfilling lives. She believes, “Life is like a car; you are the driver, and I’m the GPS guiding your journey.”

Taruni Priya Sai V,

Therapist

Taruni Priya Sai V, a psychologist who specializes in supporting adolescents and adults with anxiety, depression, burnout, and relationship issues. Using a strengths-based and person-centered approach, she creates a safe, non-judgmental space for clients to navigate their challenges. Guided by cultural sensitivity and a humanistic perspective, Taruni believes, “You’re the expert in your own life; I’m here to provide guidance and support.”

Gayatri Devi Kutcharlapati,

Therapist

Gayatri Devi Kutcharlapati, who specializes in supporting adults, adolescents, and corporate clients through concerns like depression and personality challenges. Using an eclectic approach blending Person-Centered Therapy, ACT, and trauma-informed practices, she tailors her methods to each client’s needs. Committed to empathy and active listening, Gayatri helps clients uncover their strengths and achieve lasting emotional well-being.
